My Favourite hobby


Dancing

My hobby is dancing, as it gives me joy and happiness. ... Dancing is not only an activity; it is a passion for many individuals. When I perform at parties or shows, it makes me happy, and I am delighted to appreciate people. Dancing is not only about performing moves, but it is a way to express your emotions. It improves your strength and flexibility and you would have healthier muscles and joints. Better posture and body balance if you do dancing regularly. Dancing is the gateway to different cultures in the globe. Dancing will help you to interact with new people that improves your communication skills as well. Health benefits of dancing . improved condition of your heart and lungs. .increased muscular strength, endurance and motor fitness. . increased aerobic fitness . improved muscle tone and strength . weight management .stronger bones and reduced risk of osteoporosis . better coordination, agility and flexibility.
